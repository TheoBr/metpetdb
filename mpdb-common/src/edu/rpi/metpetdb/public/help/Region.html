Region

The purpose of incorporating region names into the database is to facilitate searching for samples from a specific geographic area without having to know latitude and longitude. Prior to creating a new region for your samples, please look for other samples in the vicinity to see if a match exists.

Things to consider

  • There is no limit to the number of possible regions.
  • A sample can exist in more than one region (i.e. regions can overlap)
  • Regions should not be so small as to include only a single outcrop (? what do you think about this?)

Examples of region names

  • Adirondacks
  • Shuswap complex
  • Snowbird tectonic zone
  • Cyclades blueschist belt
  • Llano uplift
  • Abakuma terrane
  • Dalradian
  • Buchan terrane
  • Arunta block
  • Broken Hill district
  • Noshobu terrane
  • Bright Angel Canyon

You get the idea.....
