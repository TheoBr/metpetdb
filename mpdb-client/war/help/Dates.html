Formatting Dates to be read by MetPetDB bulk uploader

A date must be in a specific format in order to be recognized by MetPetDB. MM-DD-YYYY, YYYY-MM-DD or any shortened version (i.e. YYYY, or, YYYY-MM) is acceptable. Microsoft excel has a number of built in date formats, yet dates are stored as serial dates and are not reable by the MetPetDB uploader. To solve this problem, MetPetDB recommends:

  • Note that dates, in order for this to work, must be in date format in excel--year dates should have a "holder" month+day included.
    • e.g. "1979" should be changed to "01/01/1979"
  • First, create a blank column adjacent to your date column in the spreadsheet.
  • Then, convert your date in whatever format to MetPetDB format by entering in the new column ("C2" means a cell reference to your date column):
    • =TEXT(C2,"yyyy-mm-dd") for dates with specific days
    • =TEXT(C2,"yyyy-mm") for dates with just month and year information
    • =TEXT(C2,"yyyy") for dates with just the year
  • Apply these formulas to all of your dates.
  • Create a column of dates in text format by copying the entire column and "paste special" with just values to the date column.
  • Remove any unreadable columns containing formulas or other information that MetPetDB does not include.
